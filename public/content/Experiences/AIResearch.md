### Artificial Intelligence Researcher | NJIT
**Newark, New Jersey • May 2023 – March 2024**

#### **Research & Methodology Overview**
Conducted research at the **Albert Dorman Honors College** exploring machine learning applications for **gait phase estimation**. The project aimed to advance AI-powered assisted mobility devices by experimentally determining the most effective models for interpreting complex sensor data.

#### **Key Contributions & Impact**
* **Model Development & Evaluation**
    * Trained and evaluated a range of models, including **Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and Neural Networks**.
    * Utilized **Python** libraries such as **Scikit-Learn, PyTorch, and Keras** to determine the optimal approach for gait phase estimation.
* **Data Engineering & Collection**
    * Built a custom dataset by tracking motion patterns from **three IMU sensors** placed across 5+ physical locations on three test subjects.
    * Captured motion data under varying walking conditions to ensure model generalizability.
* **Optimization & Results**
    * Improved model accuracy from **92.82% to 98.65%** through iterative tuning of loss functions and **recursive feature elimination**.
    * Authored a formal research proposal documenting the experimental methodology and statistical findings.
* **End-to-End ML Lifecycle**
    * Gained mastery over the full ML lifecycle: raw data collection, feature engineering, model selection, and rigorous performance optimization.
